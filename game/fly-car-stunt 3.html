Fly Car Stunt 3

Perform daring aerial maneuvers, defy gravity and race against time

Fly Car Stunt 3 is an adrenaline-fueled racing and stunt game that takes players on a thrilling ride through futuristic tracks suspended in the sky. With its stunning 3D graphics, exhilarating gameplay, and challenging levels, Fly Car Stunt 3 offers an immersive gaming experience for fans of racing and stunt games.

Choose Your Car

Start by selecting your car from a range of futuristic vehicles available in the game. Each car has its own unique attributes such as speed, handling, and acceleration, so choose the one that best suits your racing style.

Select Your Track

Once you've chosen your car, select your track from a variety of thrilling options. Tracks may vary in difficulty, layout, and obstacles, offering different challenges and experiences for players.

Master the Controls

Familiarize yourself with the controls of the game, including steering, acceleration, and braking. Use the on-screen buttons or keyboard keys to control your car as you navigate through the tracks. 

Perform Stunts

Fly Car Stunt 3 features gravity-defying tracks with loops, ramps, and obstacles that allow you to perform incredible stunts and aerial maneuvers. Use ramps to launch your car into the air and perform flips, spins, and barrel rolls to impress the audience and earn points.

Race Against the Clock

Race against the clock as you strive to complete each track within the allotted time limit. Be quick and efficient in your movements to avoid running out of time and failing the level. 

Avoid Obstacles

Watch out for obstacles such as barriers, spikes, and gaps in the track that can hinder your progress. Use precise timing and skillful maneuvering to navigate through obstacles and reach the finish line unscathed.

Collect Coins

Collect coins scattered throughout the tracks to earn points and unlock new cars and tracks. Be on the lookout for coins placed in hard-to-reach areas or hidden behind obstacles.

Use Boosts

Collect boost power-ups scattered throughout the tracks to give your car a temporary burst of speed. Use boosts strategically to accelerate past obstacles, reach higher platforms, and shave valuable seconds off your lap time. 

Complete Challenges

Throughout the game, you'll encounter various challenges and objectives to complete. These challenges may include reaching a certain score, performing specific stunts, or completing tracks within a set time limit. Complete challenges to earn rewards and unlock new content. 

Enjoy the Experience

Most importantly, enjoy the exhilarating experience of racing and performing stunts in Fly Car Stunt 3. With its stunning visuals, challenging gameplay, and thrilling stunts, Fly Car Stunt 3 offers endless excitement and entertainment for players of all skill levels.
