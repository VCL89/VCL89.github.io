Choose a car and start the race

First, you will choose one of the available racing cars, each with its own characteristics. After choosing your car, you will start the race on one of the attractive racetracks.

Operate the car

Use the arrow keys or on-screen control buttons to drive through the track. Remember to control your speed and observe carefully to avoid collisions with obstacles and opponents.

Collect items

During the race, you will encounter item boxes on the road. Collect them to receive weapons or upgrades that increase the power and speed of your vehicle. 

Using weapons

When you have weapons, you can use them to attack opponents or protect yourself from attacks. Be careful and use tactics flexibly to gain an advantage in the race.

Complete the goal

Your goal is to surpass your opponents, complete the race and reach the finish line first. At the same time, try to control the vehicle carefully to avoid collisions and falling into a disadvantageous situation.

Upgrades and unlocks

As you complete races and earn experience points, you can use them to upgrade and unlock new vehicles, more powerful weapons, and upgraded features to improve performance. vehicle capacity.
