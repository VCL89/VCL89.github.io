Kickflip Santa

Kickflip Santa is a festive and entertaining skateboarding game that puts players in control of Santa Claus as he performs epic kickflips and tricks on his skateboard

Set against a backdrop of snowy landscapes and holiday cheer, players must guide Santa through challenging courses filled with ramps, obstacles, and festive decorations. With its charming graphics, catchy music, and addictive gameplay, Kickflip Santa offers a delightful gaming experience that captures the magic of Christmas in a fun and unique way. Strap on your helmet, grab your skateboard, and join Santa on his quest to spread joy and perform awesome tricks!

Control Santa

Use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to control Santa Claus as he rides his skateboard through each level. Navigate through snowy landscapes, urban environments, and holiday-themed obstacles as you perform kickflips and tricks.

Perform Tricks

Press the spacebar or designated button to make Santa perform a kickflip or trick while in mid-air. Experiment with different combinations of jumps, flips, and spins to rack up points and impress the audience.

Time Your Jumps

Time your jumps carefully to launch Santa off ramps and obstacles at the right moment. Aim to land on platforms and rails to maintain your momentum and continue your trick combos. 

Collect Coins

Collect coins scattered throughout the levels to earn points and unlock rewards. Coins may appear in the air, on rooftops, or hidden behind obstacles, so keep an eye out for them as you skate through each level.

Avoid Obstacles

Watch out for obstacles such as snowmen, chimneys, and Christmas decorations that can impede Santa's progress. Use quick reflexes and precise timing to navigate around obstacles and avoid crashes. 

Reach the Finish Line

Your objective in each level is to reach the finish line while collecting as many coins and performing as many tricks as possible. Use your skills to navigate through the course and complete each level within the time limit.

Unlock New Levels

As you progress through the game, you'll unlock new levels and environments to explore. Each level offers its own unique challenges and obstacles, so keep playing to discover all the festive delights Kickflip Santa has to offer.

Beat Your High Score

Challenge yourself to beat your high score with each playthrough. Experiment with different tricks and routes to maximize your points and become the ultimate skateboarding Santa champion. 

Enjoy the Festive Fun

Most importantly, enjoy the festive fun and holiday cheer of Kickflip Santa as you perform epic tricks and spread joy throughout the Christmas season. With its charming graphics, catchy music, and addictive gameplay, Kickflip Santa is sure to bring smiles to players of all ages and make Christmas extra merry and bright.
